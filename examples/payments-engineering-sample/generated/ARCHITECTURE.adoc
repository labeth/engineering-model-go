=== Functional Groups (View Scoped)


==== Payments (FG-PAYMENTS)


Description: Core payment <<REF_CATALOG_FU_CHECKOUT,checkout>> and authorization domain.



The <<REF_RT_CLUSTER_PAYMENTS,payments>> group defines the product-facing payment journey from user intent to final payment outcome.
It owns the consistency of customer messaging and transaction decisions when a payment is attempted,
declined, retried, or temporarily unavailable. The group keeps customer flow stable while depending on
fraud and external bank capabilities.



===== Payments Functional Story

<<REF_RT_CLUSTER_PAYMENTS,payments>> owns the business flow from customer intent to customer outcome. It coordinates <<REF_CATALOG_FU_CHECKOUT,checkout handling>> and <<REF_CATALOG_FU_PAYMENT_AUTHORIZATION,payment authorization>> so <<REF_CATALOG_EVT_PAYMENT_AUTH_REQUESTED,payment authorization is requested>> consistently while <<REF_CATALOG_STATE_CHECKOUT_ACTIVE,checkout is active>>. The group is responsible for keeping outcomes deterministic when <<REF_CATALOG_EVT_PAYMENT_DECLINED,payment is declined>> or when <<REF_CATALOG_EVT_BANK_LINK_UNAVAILABLE,bank link is unavailable>>.



==== Fraud Evaluation (FG-FRAUD)


Description: Risk scoring and fraud audit domain.



The <<REF_CATALOG_FG_FRAUD,fraud evaluation>> group exists to reduce unsafe approvals while preserving throughput for legitimate <<REF_RT_CLUSTER_PAYMENTS,payments>>.
It combines automated <<REF_CATALOG_FU_RISK_SCORING,risk scoring>> with manual <<REF_CATALOG_FU_SUPPORT_REVIEW,support review>> so uncertain transactions move through a controlled
escalation path. The group ensures <<REF_RT_NAMESPACE_RISK,risk>> decisions are explainable, auditable, and operationally actionable.



===== Fraud Evaluation Functional Story

<<REF_CATALOG_FG_FRAUD,fraud evaluation>> turns uncertain transaction <<REF_RT_NAMESPACE_RISK,risk>> into actionable decisions for <<REF_CATALOG_FU_PAYMENT_AUTHORIZATION,payment authorization>>. It provides <<REF_CATALOG_FU_RISK_SCORING,risk scoring>> and <<REF_CATALOG_FU_SUPPORT_REVIEW,support review>> so high-risk flows are handled as <<REF_CATALOG_STATE_REVIEW_PENDING,payment review is pending>> instead of forcing unsafe automatic outcomes. The group must preserve clear transitions from automated scoring to manual handling when <<REF_CATALOG_EVT_SUPPORT_NOTIFIED,support agent is notified>>.



==== Platform (FG-PLATFORM)


Description: Cluster provisioning and GitOps operations domain.



The platform group provides the runtime and deployment foundation used by all product capabilities.
It owns environment provisioning, namespace and cluster boundaries, and continuous reconciliation behavior.
The group enables safe delivery and reliable operations without owning payment business logic.



===== Platform Functional Story

platform provides the execution foundation and delivery control needed by <<REF_RT_CLUSTER_PAYMENTS,payments>> and <<REF_CATALOG_FG_FRAUD,fraud evaluation>>. It includes <<REF_CATALOG_FU_CLUSTER_PROVISIONING,cluster provisioning>> and <<REF_CATALOG_FU_GITOPS_OPERATIONS,gitops operations>> as explicit architecture capabilities, not background concerns. The group supports <<REF_CATALOG_MODE_NORMAL,normal mode is active>> operations while enabling controlled lifecycle changes.
